A new and innovative male cosmetic brand, aspires to embody timeless elegance through its products.

ABOUT ONE MOMENT

One Moment – skin care believes in a transformative power of self-care and the beauty found in simplicity. The brand is dedicated to crafting skincare products that capture the essence of timeless elegance and sophistication. With a commitment to quality, natural ingredients, and the belief that every moment devoted to personal care is precious, One Moment Skin Care offers a range of products that empower individuals to embrace their skin care routines with confidence.

THE GOAL

The goal of this branding project is to develop a comprehensive visual identity for One Moment - a skincare brand targeting male grooming. The branding will encompass the design of a distinctive logo, cosmetic bottle concept, elegant packaging, typography selection, and a nude earth tones color palette. The brand aims to convey minimalism, timeless elegance, and the importance of taking a moment for self-care.

THE PROCESS

The user research and discovery phase employed online surveys, social media analysis, interviews, and market analysis to gain insights into men’s skincare habits and preferences. This research led to the development of personas representing busy professionals, fitness enthusiasts, and first-time users. Findings were synthesized through affinity mapping and empathy mapping, revealing key user motivations and pain points that guided the design direction.

THE RESULTS

The project resulted in a visually distinctive and emotionally resonant brand identity that aligned with users’ preferences for simplicity and minimalism. The innovative clock-like bottle design became a standout feature, effectively conveying the brand’s philosophy of dedicating time to self-care. Through continuous user testing, a user-centered design was achieved, ensuring the product experience was functional, and memorable.

KEY FEATURES

USER-CENTRIC RESEARCH

The research phase involved online surveys, social media analysis, interviews, and a comprehensive market analysis aimed at uncovering men’s skincare habits and preferences. Based on these insights, personas were developed to represent busy professionals, fitness enthusiasts, and first-time users. To capture detailed user pain points and motivations, affinity mapping and empathy mapping were applied, organizing findings into actionable insights that shaped the design approach.

BRAND IDENTITY DESIGN

A cohesive concept was developed to align with the vision of timeless elegance. Visual elements included:

Logo - Represented time with elegant typography, evoking sophistication and masculinity.

Typography - Bold yet minimalistic, conveying confidence.

Color Palette - Nude earth tones to evoke calm and masculinity.

Bottle Design - Clock-like concept emphasizing the importance of taking “one moment” for skincare.

PACKAGING CONCEPT

The bottle design was developed to uniquely showcase the brand's products, featuring an innovative clock-like arrangement when the two main cream products are placed together. This design emphasizes the brand’s message of dedicating 'one moment' to a skincare routine.

The packaging was crafted to embody minimalist elegance, combining clean lines with a focus on functionality. An earthy color palette was incorporated to convey a natural and organic essence, seamlessly balancing a luxurious look and feel.

ITERATIVE TESTING

Branding concepts, including colors, logos, and typography, were initially tested with focus groups to gauge emotional responses. Feedback guided iterative refinements, resulting in a final brand identity that resonated with users across digital, print, and packaging mediums. Additionally, a unified social media strategy was established to reinforce the brand’s values and dedication to men’s skincare, ensuring emotional engagement and strong recognition across platforms.

Next Steps and Learning

The skincare company plans to launch the improved skincare line for men in Europe with a focus on digital platforms for a seamless user experience. The key takeaways from this case study include the importance of thorough user research, persona creation, empathy mapping, and rigorous user testing in creating a successful UX design. In the future, ongoing user feedback will be crucial to adapt to evolving user needs and market trends. Additionally, the product line and design will be continuously refined to provide the best skincare solutions for European men, ensuring long-term success and user satisfaction.
