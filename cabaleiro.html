CABALEIRO

Cabaleiro is a mobile app that offers the latest in fashion, combining seamless shopping with curated collections to elevate fashion style effortlessly.

Date: 07.2020
Client: CABALEIRO
Category: Fashion
Task: Redesign of the Account Section
A Fashion Industry App Store - This project involved redesigning the account section for Cabaleiro, a dedicated app store for the fashion industry. The goal was to enhance user experience by creating a seamless and visually appealing interface that reflects Cabaleiro’s brand identity and meets the unique needs of its fashion-focused audience.

Phase 1: BRAINSTORMING

In the initial stage, I focused on brainstorming ideas based on user needs and industry trends, gathering insights to set a clear direction for the account section. By sketching out various layout concepts, I could quickly explore possibilities for structuring features like order history, profile settings, and wishlists. This phase allowed for flexibility and creativity, helping to identify the most effective components and layout ideas. Through a structured, three-phase approach, the redesign focused on creating an intuitive, engaging experience that aligns with the latest design standards in e-commerce and digital branding.

Phase 2: PROTOTYPING

Once a direction was clear, I translated sketches into low-fidelity wireframes to organize the overall structure and navigation. This phase focused on functionality without distraction from design details. Using a simple grayscale layout, I developed a prototype to test user interactions and ensure that each element of the account section served a purpose, providing users with a seamless and intuitive experience.

Phase 3: HI-FI WIREFRAMES

With feedback and insights gathered, I moved on to high-fidelity wireframes, incorporating the final design elements, brand colors, and typography that reflect Cabaleiro’s modern aesthetic. In this final step, every visual detail was polished to align with Cabaleiro’s branding, resulting in a cohesive, stylish, and functional account section that enhances the user experience. The final solution offers a seamless and elegant interface that complements the brand's image and makes navigating the account section effortless for users.
