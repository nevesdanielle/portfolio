YCO

Children's Lighting

YCO is a green design light for kids. The base is a fitting vacuum/suction that can mount on any flat surface. Its main structure has a twist switch system with 2 mood LED lights (day and night), providing a 360 degrees light direction for indoor use. The light is very playful connecting multiple portablee sets at once. Yco uses rechargeable lithium battery and is made of recyclable polymer.


Goal

To create a playingfull, colorfull, non-toxic and recyclable children lamp.


Inspiration

Y Water is a vitamin-infused organic drink targeted at kids with a unique bottle designed by Yves Behar.The bottle also has a second life as a toy using connectors that come with each bottle to join with other bottles.


Concept Solution

Yco is made of individual lights connecting together and illuminating with a simple twist. The parts can be enjoyed from across the room or just easily, playfully rearranged to create unique designs and shapes.
