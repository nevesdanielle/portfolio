SMAP

In an industry where real-time data and precise tracking are paramount, SMAP sought to develop a cutting-edge logistics platform to empower transportation companies and end-users with enhanced control over their supply chains.

Designed with a deep understanding of the logistics sector’s unique demands, the SMAP platform integrates both a web and mobile application to streamline operations and provide comprehensive, real-time insights into critical data such as location tracking, movement, and environmental conditions.

Task: Branding and UI Design
Role: UI/UX Designer
Tools: Figma, Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Balsamiq Mockups, SolidWorks, HTML, CSS, JS

THE GOAL

This project aimed to develop a logistics platform solution tailored specifically for SMAP. The platform aimed to empower transportation companies, senders, and end-users by offering real-time insights and comprehensive control over logistics data, including tracking, movement, and environmental conditions. The goal was to deliver a user-friendly web and mobile application capable of meeting the critical demands of the logistics industry. By combining intuitive design with advanced functionality, the platform was designed to streamline operations and improve decision-making across the supply chain.

THE PROCESS

Developing SMAP's logistics platform required a comprehensive approach, beginning with an in-depth exploration of customer pain points and validation of value propositions specific to the logistics industry. Extensive research, including user interviews and competitor analysis, uncovered critical insights into needs such as tracking, movement control, and environmental monitoring, shaping the foundational requirements for the platform. Initial prototypes were crafted to outline key functionalities, such as real-time tracking, sensor monitoring, and alert notifications, which enabled early feedback to refine usability and streamline navigation.

THE RESULTS

The project resulted in a robust, user-centered logistics platform that provided seamless control and visibility into critical supply chain data for all stakeholders. By the end of the project, SMAP’s platform offered real-time insights into tracking, sensor monitoring, and other essential logistics functions, setting a new standard for usability in the industry. The final design was visually appealing, functionally effective, and aligned with SMAP’s branding goals, enhancing both user engagement and operational efficiency. Additionally, the platform’s streamlined interface positioned SMAP, a startup in the logistics sector, as an attractive contender for investors.

RESEARCH AND INVESTIGATION

Objective: Understand user pain points, validate value propositions, and identify opportunities for market differentiation.

Actions: Conducted user interviews and competitor analysis, focusing on logistics industry leaders to understand their strengths and areas for improvement. Explored potential revenue models, customer needs, and foundational requirements to create a solution that would address both user needs and market gaps.

PROTOTYPING AND WIREFRAMING

Objective: Develop functional, user-centered prototypes for usability testing and validation.

Actions: Created low-fidelity wireframes to design basic layouts and establish the flow of core functionalities, including real-time tracking, sensor monitoring, and notifications. Based on user feedback, refined these designs into high-fidelity wireframes, ensuring that navigation and functionality were aligned with user expectations and industry standards.

TESTING AND IMPLEMENTATION

Objective: Finalize the design with high-quality, industry-aligned visuals and ensure it meets user needs.

Actions: High-fidelity prototypes incorporated brand-specific color schemes, typography, and detailed design elements. The final design was presented to stakeholders, showcasing its alignment with user expectations and its potential to attract future investors and meet market demands.
