ONE MOMENT

Elevating Your Skin Care Experience

This case study focuses on improving the user experience for a skincare line targeting men in the European market. The product line includes a moisturizer, a tonic, and sunblock. The company is dedicated to providing the best skincare solutions for its clients by understanding their needs, preferences, and pain points. This study covers the entire UX design process, from user research to testing and iteration.

One Moment Skin Care believes in a transformative power of self-care and the beauty found in simplicity. The brand is dedicated to crafting skincare products that capture the essence of timeless elegance and sophistication. With a commitment to quality, natural ingredients, and the belief that every moment devoted to personal care is precious, One Moment Skin Care offers a range of products that empower individuals to embrace their skin care routines with confidence. The "One Moment" branding identity was developed with the intention of capturing the essence of fleeting, impactful experiences. The project aimed to craft a visually cohesive identity that could evoke feelings of serenity, mindfulness, and focus. The goal was to create a brand that resonates with users seeking meaningful interactions in a fast-paced world, ensuring that each visual element speaks to the idea of 'presence' and 'awareness.


Problem Statement

One Moment, a new male cosmetic brand, is seeking to establish its presence in the market but lacks a clear concept and comprehensive design strategy to kickstart its operations. The company faces challenges in packaging design, product aesthetics, social media branding, and overall user experience. Without a strong brand identity and user-friendly online presence, One Moment struggles to differentiate itself and connect with potential customers effectively.


Research and Discovery Phase

The initial phase involved a deep dive into competitive analysis, where brands in wellness, mindfulness, and self-care were examined. User research revealed a strong need for a brand identity that doesnâ€™t overwhelm with visuals but rather fosters an emotional connection through simplicity and minimalism. This insight shaped the approach to favor clean design, soft color palettes, and subtle, purposeful typography.

To better understand the target audience, we conducted both quantitative and qualitative research. Methods included:

Online Surveys: Gathered data on men's skincare habits, preferences, and pain points.

Social Media Analysis: Identified trends and user sentiments.

Interviews: Conducted in-depth interviews to gain deeper insights and personal experiences, supplementing surveys and recruiting volunteers through social media channels to distribute questionnaires among users.

Market Analysis: Analyzed market trends and competitors in the European skincare industry, by accessing pre-built testing databases.


Ideation Steps

We created personas based on our research data to understand the needs and goals of our target users. Within a limited scope, the personas included busy professionals, fitness enthusiasts, and first-time users.

Affinity Mapping

Usability Testing

- Survey: To capture initial perceptions and expectations.

- Daily Usage Log: Participants record their experience after each use.

- Post-Use Survey and Interview: To collect detailed feedback on overall satisfaction, observed benefits, and any issues.


Duration: 2-4 weeks to allow users to observe and report on the moisturizer's effects and experience over time.



Aspects Considered

Effectiveness: How well the moisturizer hydrates and meets skincare needs.

Texture and Absorption: How the moisturizer feels on the skin and how quickly it absorbs.

Scent: The strength and appeal of the fragrance.

Packaging: Ease of use, practicality, and visual appeal of the container.

Application Experience: Overall ease and satisfaction with the application process.

Skin Feel Post-Application: How the skin feels immediately and several hours after application.





Execution

Recruitment: Used diverse channels like gyms, campuses, and online platforms to find participants fitting each profile.

Distribution: Provided the product with detailed usage instructions and a diary/log for recording experiences.

Feedback Collection: Used a combination of online surveys and follow-up phone interviews to gather comprehensive insights.



Solution Statement

By understanding our users through personas and empathy maps, and validating our designs through iterative testing, we can confidently launch a skincare line that meets the unique needs of European men, ensuring they feel valued and catered to.

One Moment's design project aims to enhance branding and user experience by developing a complete visual identity that reflects minimalism and timeless elegance, important for men's skincare routines. This includes creating cosmetic bottles, a distinctive logo, elegant packaging, selecting typography, and defining a color palette, tailored to meet user needs as the company prepares for launch.

Logo Design: To craft a logo that seamlessly integrates the concept of time with elegant, bold typography. The logo should evoke a sense of sophistication and masculinity while reflecting the brand's commitment to quality and self-care.

Typography: To select bold yet minimalistic typography that aligns with the brand's Scandinavian aesthetic. The typography should be easily legible and convey a sense of confidence and sophistication.

Color Palette: To curate a nude earth color palette that includes warm, natural tones. These colors will evoke a sense of calm, masculinity, and timelessness while harmonizing with the brand's commitment to natural ingredients.

Bottle Cosmetic Concept: To develop a unique bottle design concept that not only showcases the brand's products but also assembles to form a clock-like circle when two main cream products are placed together. This concept will emphasize the importance of taking "one moment" for skin care routine.

Packaging Design: To create packaging that exudes minimalist elegance, with clean lines and a focus on functionality. To incorporate the earthy color palette to convey a natural and organic feel while maintaining a luxurious look and feel.

Social Media Branding: To establish a consistent and captivating social media presence that showcases the brand's products, values, and commitment to male skin care routine.



Results




Next Steps and Learning

The skincare company plans to launch the improved skincare line for men in Europe with a focus on digital platforms for a seamless user experience. The key takeaways from this case study include the importance of thorough user research, persona creation, empathy mapping, and rigorous user testing in creating a successful UX design. In the future, ongoing user feedback will be crucial to adapt to evolving user needs and market trends. Additionally, the product line and design will be continuously refined to provide the best skincare solutions for European men, ensuring long-term success and user satisfaction.
